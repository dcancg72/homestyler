## Tips for Getting the Most Out of It

After spending way too much time playing with this platform, here are some tricks:

**Start with Templates**: Don't reinvent the wheel. The template library has hundreds of pre-made rooms you can modify. It's faster than starting from scratch, and you can learn by seeing how professional designers set things up.

**Use the AR Feature**: Before you buy expensive furniture, use the AR preview to make sure it actually fits your space. That sofa might look perfect in the 3D render but completely overwhelm your actual living room.

**Join Design Challenges**: The platform runs weekly challenges with specific themes. It's a good way to push yourself creatively and see what other users are doing. Plus, winners get free membership perks.

**Organize Your Projects**: Create separate folders for different rooms or clients. Future you will be grateful when you're not scrolling through 47 "untitled_design_final_v2" projects.

**Export Early and Often**: Save renders and floor plans as you go. The platform is cloud-based and generally reliable, but internet hiccups happen. Having local copies of your work prevents panic attacks.

**Leverage the Community**: The forum and gallery are full of people solving the same problems you're facing. Can't figure out how to create curved stairs? Someone's already posted a tutorial.
